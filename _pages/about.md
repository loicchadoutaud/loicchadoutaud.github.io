I am an engineer from [Mines Paris PSL](https://www.minesparis.psl.eu/) and PhD graduate in machine learning and computational biology. My doctoral research was supervised by [Thomas Walter](https://thomaswalter.github.io) and [Emmanuel Barillot](https://curie.fr/personne/emmanuel-barillot) at [Institut Curie](https://curie.fr/).

My PhD work focused on developing machine learning methods for both high-dimensional transcriptomic data and pathology imaging.

My main research interests lie at the intersection of self-supervised learning, multimodal learning, and computational biology.
